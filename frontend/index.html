Our Story

Bringing South India
to Your Doorstep

Sri Gokul Foods was born from a simple passion — sharing the authentic flavours of South Indian cuisine with the people of Hyderabad. Named after Lord Krishna's divine abode, our restaurant embodies purity, tradition, and devotion to quality.

Every item on our menu is prepared using recipes passed down through generations. We believe great food is made with love, fresh ingredients, and an honest heart. From crispy dosas to flavourful chaats — every bite is a journey.
